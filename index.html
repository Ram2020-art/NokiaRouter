    myWorldLink Connection Guide

    For Android Users:

    1. Go to Wi-Fi Settings and select the SSID "myWorldLink".
    2. If available, click on "Advanced Options".
    3. Select "PEAP" in the EAP method.
    4. Select "MSCHAPV2" in Phase 2 authentication.
    5. Choose "Do not validate" or "Unspecified" in the CA certificate field.
    6. Type your WorldLink account username in the Identity field.
    7. In the Password section, enter your WorldLink Online Service password.
    8. Click "CONNECT".

    For iPhone/Windows/PC Users:

    1. Go to Settings and select Wi-Fi.
    2. Select the SSID "myWorldLink" from the list.
    3. Enter your WorldLink account Username & Password.
    4. Click "CONNECT".

    For Non-WorldLink Customers:

    1. Select the SSID "Free_WorldLink_Wi-Fi".
    2. Click on "Get Online".
    3. Click on "Connect on Free Wi-Fi".
    4. Click on "Are you a new user?".
    5. Insert your mobile number and click "Next".
    6. Confirm your mobile number and click "Continue".
    7. Enter the OTP received via SMS and click "Login".
